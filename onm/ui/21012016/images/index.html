Offer Near Me - Mockup UI

  • 01. Splash-Screen

    This is the welcome screen when opening the app.

  • 02. User-Detail

    On this page the user has to provide his/her details which includes Name and Email address.

  • 03. Home-screen

    The Home-Screen shows various categories, viewed by scrolling from bottom to top with product images, user ratings and discounts offered.

  • 04.Food-Category

    This page provides offers on ordering food online or discount coupons on a meal from a nearby restaurant while providing customer review ratings.

  • 05.Accessories-Category

    This page has information on the latest discounts on various accessories ranging from clothes, shoes, watches, sunglasses to electrical gadgets.

  • 06. Product-Detail

    This page highlights the offer details of the product along with the product image and location details to avail the offer.

  • 07. Direction

    The map gives a detailed route from the location of the user to the place where the offer on the product can be availed from.
